Payment & Funding Methods

Mobile Banking (I.D. Verified)

Approved platforms include PayPal®, Chime®, ApplePay®, Zelle®, CashApp®, Varo®, and Venmo® (subject to availability). Government-issued I.D. required. Funds must clear prior to bond filing.

Retail Cash & Debit Funding

In-store deposits, reloadable debit options, and barcode-based funding supported. Receipt confirmation mandatory.

Card Processing

Visa®™ & Mastercard®™ accepted subject to account review. Billing verification required. Processing fees may apply.

MoneyGram Transfers

Reference number and sender verification required prior to bond processing.

All payments subject to verification and underwriting approval.
